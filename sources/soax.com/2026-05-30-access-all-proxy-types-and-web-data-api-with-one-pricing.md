###### How often does the IP rotate?

We provide flexible IP rotation options to fit your requirements. You can configure the rotation settings through your dashboard.

All proxy types offer IP rotation on each new request, or at a set interval that suits you. The maximum amount of time between IP refreshes is one hour for mobile and residential proxies, or up to 24 hours for datacenter and ISP proxies.

###### What's the difference between residential and mobile proxies?

**Residential proxies** are IP addresses associated with real residential users and devices connected to home internet services (like Wi-Fi). They are highly legitimate and suitable for a wide range of web scraping and data collection tasks.

**Mobile proxies** are IP addresses linked to mobile devices using cellular data networks (3G, 4G, 5G). These are considered highly trustworthy by many online services due to their nature and frequent IP changes within the mobile carrier's range, making them ideal for accessing challenging targets.
