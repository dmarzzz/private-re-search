# Google's IP Protection Announcement

As a forward-looking entity in the realm of data and privacy, Throtle is committed to ensuring our clients and partners stay informed about industry shifts and evolutions that could affect their operations. ### Google's IP Protection Overview

Google's proposal centers around increasing user privacy by sending third-party traffic for specific domains through proxies. The goal is to mask users' IP addresses, thus offering them heightened security and privacy. While this initiative emphasizes user control over their data by making it opt-in initially, Google is notably proceeding with caution, emphasizing a phased rollout (similar to other Google rollouts).
