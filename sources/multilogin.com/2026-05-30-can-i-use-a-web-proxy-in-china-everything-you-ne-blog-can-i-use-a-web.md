**How the Great Firewall of China Blocks Websites**

The **Great Firewall of China** is a combination of laws, regulations, and technological systems designed to **control internet access** within the country. It blocks foreign websites using several methods:

**IP Blocking**– The firewall maintains a blacklist of IP addresses associated with banned websites and services.**DNS Filtering**– Websites are blocked by manipulating DNS queries, making it impossible for users to access them through normal means.**Deep Packet Inspection (DPI)**– The firewall analyzes internet traffic to identify and block proxy and VPN traffic.**Keyword Censorship**– Messages containing sensitive political or controversial keywords are filtered and blocked.

Because of this **multi-layered censorship system**, **regular web proxies and many VPNs struggle to work in China**.
